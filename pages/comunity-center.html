Our Space

The space of the Vestigium association is a versatile place designed to foster collaboration, learning, and well-being. It consists of two rooms that can be used by community members for various activities. The first room is a multifunctional area with an expandable table, a small library with books in different languages, and a bar with a kitchen equipped for preparing hot beverages. This space is ideal for coworking, workshops, talks, and cultural gatherings. The second room is set up for low-impact physical activities such as yoga or meditation and features a mirrored wall and a storage area. The space is available to anyone interested in contributing to or participating in community activities, promoting interaction and knowledge exchange in a welcoming and inclusive environment
