Projects in Colaboration with the City of Zagreb

Sustainable Future

**Sustainable Future - The Path to Healthy Food** promotes regenerative agriculture, short supply chains, and school gardens as educational tools. Through education, workshops, and practical examples, it encourages sustainable farming, strengthens local producer-consumer connections, and engages students in growing their own food. The project aims to raise community awareness about the need for a more sustainable and healthy food system.

Learn more

Club for the Elderly

Vrbani lacks socio-cultural institutions for the elderly, so we aim to provide a high-quality program tailored to their needs. After listening to their interests through personal interactions, we have designed activities specifically for retirees and elderly individuals. The program is intended for retirees and elderly individuals through the following activities:
Atelier - Various creative workshops, from painting to weaving.
Social Hub - Communication workshops, board games, ecology, and technology.

Learn more

Independent Cultural Center

We organize a socio-cultural program through the Independent Cultural Center, which operates within the Vrbani Community Center through the following activities:
Vestigium 50+.
Creative program for children during holidays.
Culture and activism.
Since 2011, when we started working in Vrbani, we realized how the lack of cultural content in the neighborhood affects citizens' lives. This was one of the key reasons why we founded the association: to create a place in the neighborhood for gatherings, social interactions, and exchanges.
